About ConstruxFlow

ConstruxFlow is a comprehensive construction management platform that streamlines project coordination between contractors, workers, and suppliers. Manage projects, hire workers, order materials, and track everything in one place.

Features

For Contractors

Manage projects, hire workers, order materials, and track progress all in one dashboard.

For Workers

Browse job opportunities, apply to projects, and manage your work schedule efficiently.

For Suppliers

Manage inventory, receive orders, and connect with contractors for material supply.

Rating System

Build trust through our comprehensive rating and review system.

Real-time Updates

Stay informed with instant notifications for applications, orders, and approvals.

Easy to Use

Simple, intuitive interface designed for the construction industry.
